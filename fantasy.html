The Hidden World of Harry Potter

After years of being treated like a servant by the Dursleys, Harry discovers he is a wizard. On his eleventh birthday he is rescued by Rubeus Hagrid, who reveals the secret of Harry's past and invites him to Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry.

The Final Reveal: Courage and love are his greatest weapons.
