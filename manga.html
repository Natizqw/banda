Plot

The Apothecary in the Imperial Palace

The setting is a fictional country called Li, reminiscent of medieval China. The main heroine is 17-year-old Maomao, the adopted daughter of an apothecary, who is highly knowledgeable about medicines, diseases, and poisons.

She is kidnapped and sold as a maid to the imperial palace, where her expertise catches the attention of the chief eunuch Renshi, and she becomes a personal poison taster for the emperor's senior consorts.

Setting

The story takes place inside a vast imperial palace inspired by imperial China. The inner palace, where the emperor's consorts reside, is a world of its own — full of politics, poison, and hidden secrets that only Maomao dares to uncover.
