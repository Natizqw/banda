plot

A Family on the Run Across the Universe

Saga tells the story of Alana and Marko, two soldiers from opposite sides of a never-ending galactic war who fall in love and have a child together. Their daughter Hazel is born during a desperate escape — and narrates the story from some point in her future.

Their home planets, Landfall and its moon Wreath, have been at war for generations. Rather than fight directly, both sides now wage proxy wars across the galaxy. When Alana and Marko's union is discovered, both governments send deadly agents to hunt them down.

Key conflict: Can a family survive a universe determined to tear them apart?

setting

The story spans dozens of alien worlds, space stations, and uncharted planets. Landfall is a massive technologically advanced planet whose inhabitants have wings. Its moon Wreath is home to a magical culture whose people have horns.

The galaxy is crowded with mercenaries, royalty, ghosts, giant tortoises used as spaceships, and tabloid journalists — all of whom cross paths with the young family as they search for a safe place to raise their daughter.
